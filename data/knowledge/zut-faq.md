# ZUT Student Support — Seed FAQ

Official reference content for the RAG knowledge base (Appendix A test cases).

## Fees

Tuition for the Diploma in Information Technology (DIT) is **K7,445.00 per semester**.

## Student ID

If you lost your ID card, visit the **Helpdesk**. Replacement fee: **K350**.

## Admissions

Applications close **30th November 2026**.

Entry requirements for Diploma in Information Technology: **5 O-Level credits including English and Mathematics**.
